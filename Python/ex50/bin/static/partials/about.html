This is a project by the students of Lappeenranta University of Technology (LUT), Finland. It implements the concept of engagement of citizen of Lappeenranta for detecting foul smells in the city. The project is funded by LUT

This is a platform where citizens can share their feeling about the odour they sense in any place of the city. The data shared by the citizens will be provided to the Government officials. This helps in identifying the issues like forest fire or a gas leakage or a contaminated trash place easily.
