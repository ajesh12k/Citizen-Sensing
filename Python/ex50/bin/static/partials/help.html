Common questions

What is CSense?

CSense is an opportunity for people of Lappeenranta to show their care for the city environment and help city officials to track environment degradation in real time. This platform allows citizens to report various issues and city officials to track them.

How to use it?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Connect the Bluetooth clicker with your Mobile's Bluetooth
  • Open Citizen Sensing Application.
  • Register your device with a device name that you can easily remember.
  • Press the button on clicker and allow location permissions.
  • Select appropriate issue type to be reported
  • Press again to report an issue

Who can use it?

Any individual who wants to report an issue in and around the city can use it.
System questions

What is the system requirement?

The system requirement is pretty simple. All you need is:
  • A Bluetooth Clicker
  • An android phone
  • An active Internet connection

What can I report?

You can report many things surrounding you with this application:




  • You can report bad smell in the environment
  • Ice on lake
  • Algae formation
  • Calm places
  • Alien species

From where can I get the Bluetooth device?

The device can be requested from the Lappeenranta City Officials.

Do I have to pay?

No! Currently, the devices are free for enthusiastic reporters.

How it effects my privacy?

This platform is completely safe!

You personal information, such as Name, Address, Email id or other social media information is not required by this platform. The location information is not tagged to an individual, but the device name that you give at the time of registration.

What if I loose the device?

You can report the device as lost from the web page of the application.

Is it available only in Lappeenranta?

Currently, the city officials of Lappeenranta will be looking for the reported issues and will be taking steps to resolve them. However, the application can be used anywhere.
